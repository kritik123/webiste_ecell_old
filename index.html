About Us

Keeping this idea in mind, we at the E-Cell OCT, focus on providing a platform and environment to young ideas so that they can grow and shine bright at the current competitive market. We want to be a budding tree, whose fruit can be a success mantra for the students and faculties.
Our motive is to nourish and create entrepreneur to a level that they learn and believe that “entrepreneurs never quit”. We make them learn that being an entrepreneur is not very easy but even in the darkness they should move so that they can turn the light on. We want them to believe in themselves and give a try because in the either way ship is not going to sink.
Through our conference, social events, lectures, speaker engagements and workshops, we wish to bring together everyone interested in the startup world and to help students to best utilize resources and find or join startups.

Vision

The Entrepreneurship Cell, OCT Bhopal is a platform for the aspiring young minds who wish to pursue entrepreneurship and reach the pinnacle of glory. The E-Cell aims at manifesting the essence of entrepreneurship in the convoluting gray matters of the young force coming up. This delegation gives wings to ideas and support in the form of investors and seed funding along with mentorship guidance to the pioneering and budding entrepreneurs. The E-Cell aims at promoting the startup culture and building up entrepreneurs who have it in them to become the greatest pioneers of this century.
